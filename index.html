About Me

Hi, I’m Isaac Oluwole Adigun, known as Ephzibah Behulah. I’m a Cybersecurity student at IU Internationale Hochschule, Germany, with a background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Nigeria. By day, I work as an Elektroniker at Centerline Design, Hamburg, contributing to system installations for Airbus aircraft. By night, I dive into Cybersecurity and Data Analytics, studying at ReDI School Hamburg.

My journey in tech is driven by a passion for ethical digital systems, AI in cybersecurity, and creating a resilient online world. I’m honing skills in Python, BigQuery, Google Colab, and network security, while embracing agile methodologies like Scrum. I share my learning journey through GitHub, GitBook, and my blog, reflecting my commitment to growth and transparency.
